  • His mother was very religious and forbid him from listening to hip hop.
  • Juice Wrld started his rapping career from soundcloud.
  • His name originally was "Juice The Kid" before his rise in popularity
  • Juice Wrld became popular in 2018 when hit singles "Lucid Dreams" and "All Girls Are the Same" came out.
